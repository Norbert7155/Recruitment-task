Room Renovation Plan

1. Clean the Room
    Remove clutter and throw away things you don't need.
    Dust all furniture, vacuum the carpet, and mop the floor.
    Take down posters and decorations.
    Remove electrical sockets.

2. Prepare Surfaces for Painting
    Cover the Floor and Windows
    Remove any old paint.
    Fill holes, repair cracks, and sand the walls smooth.
    Clean the walls of dust and dirt.

3. Painting
    Choose a color you like.
    Buy paint and paint rollers.
    Paint the walls evenly with two coats, letting each coat dry.

4. Arranging Furniture
    Plan a layout for the room.
    Bring in furniture with the help of friends or family.
    Add final touches like lamps, pictures and flowers.
